Raw Weather Reports

Raw weather data from a stand-alone weather station is contained in the Information Field of an APRS AX.25 frame:

Raw Weather Report Format
  ! or  
  # or Raw Weather Data
  $ or  
  *  
Bytes: 1 n

Examples

Ultimeter 2000:
!!006B005803500000—-03E9——–002105140000005D (Ultimeter 2000)
Peet Bros U-II:
#50B7500820082
Ultimeter 2000:
$ULTW0031003702CE0069—-000086A00001—-011901CC00000005
Peet Bros U-II:
*7007600000000
